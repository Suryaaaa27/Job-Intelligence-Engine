Interior Commercial Superintendent Job Opportunity — $140k - $185k + Benefits + Bonuses + Career Growth

If you’re a Superintendent who leads from the front, solves problems in the field, and delivers tough work safely and on schedule, this is where you belong. Our client is a full‑service construction firm that has built a strong reputation for delivering a consistently high standard of service across corporate, retail, and specialty projects for over 30 years. Collaboration defines how the team operates. Close partnerships with clients, architects, subcontractors, and vendors foster clear communication, strategic planning, and coordinated execution.


What we're looking for:

  • 6+ years of experience in corporate interior, amenity spaces, lobby projects, and construction projects between $5M - $25M from start to finish.

  • A proven track record of successfully overseeing on-site construction to ensure projects are completed on schedule.

  • Strong field leadership skills with the ability to coordinate subcontractors and manage daily operations.
